Notice, please read

Not for navigation. This app is not intended as a substitute for a weather briefing obtained from a Flight Service Station (1-800-WXBRIEF). Currently, the information contained here does not meet the FAA requirements for a pre-flight weather brief. Therefore, it's important that pilots still call and obtain a briefing from an FAA Flight Service Specialist.

Nearby Metars retrieves METAR data from reporting locations within a 50 nautical mile radius of your current location. To view the METAR raw text, tap on the icon.

You can also scroll pan the map and get METAR data around the location centered on the screen, and clicking on "Get Metars" menu item.

Cloud coverage icon

The cloud coverage icons are based on the ADDS METAR cloud coverage. See the ADDS METARs Help Page for more information

Wind speed will be indicated in the wind barb for speeds up to 100kts.

Viewing raw METAR text

Tapping on the icon will display the raw METAR text. Press your phone's back button to close the METAR screen
